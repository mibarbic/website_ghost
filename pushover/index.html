Pushover - Maggery [MV]

5 December 2021 / Projects

About:

Pushover isn't your average music video, it's a psychological mind bending take on abusive relationships. Created for rising bedroom pop artist Maggery.

Specification:

Format: Digital Video | 2:45 | 1080P

Awards: 2D3D+ Exhibition ‘19 - Selected Work

Client: @maggery

Role: Creative Director, Cinematographer, Motion Designer, Color Grader, Audio Mixer, and Video Editor.

Problem:

Artist @maggery has a song and needs a visualization of it.

Solution:

Produce a visually interesting music video with an underlying narrative related to the lyrics and beliefs of the artist to engage the audience.

Result:

A well-received music video that brought more engagement and exposure to the artist.

Credits:

Song Credit: Pushover - Maggery

On-Screen Talent: Maggie Huang, Gordon Phung, Penguin Plush (RIP).
